Privacy Policy – Apps by App Bridge Vietnam

Thank you for using our mobile applications (“Apps”). This Privacy Policy explains how our Apps handle information. It applies to all Apps published by App Bridge Vietnam that do not require user accounts, do not collect personal data, and may display advertising via Google AdMob.

1. Information We Collect

Our Apps do not collect, store, or share personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contacts
  • GPS or location
  • Microphone or camera data
  • Device identifiers

All user-generated content (e.g., personal notes, lists, categories, reminders) is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this information to servers or external services.

2. Advertising and Google AdMob

Some of our Apps use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process device-related data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. This data is handled by Google according to their Privacy Policy.

Depending on your region (e.g., EU/EEA/UK), the App may display a consent form provided by Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP). You can choose:

  • Personalized ads
  • Non-personalized ads
  • Continue without consent

Your choice is stored only for advertising purposes by AdMob and handled according to Google policies. Our Apps do not access or process this data.

Google AdMob privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Third-Party Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.
  • We do not use external analytics or tracking services.
  • The only third-party service is Google AdMob for advertising.

4. Permissions

Our Apps do not request sensitive permissions such as:

  • Camera
  • Contacts
  • GPS / Location
  • Microphone

If a specific App requests storage permission (for export or backup), it is used only to store or read files on your device. We do not upload or share this data.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our Apps do not knowingly collect or process personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our Apps, please contact us so we can assist.

6. Data Security

Because the Apps do not send data to external servers, all information remains on your device. You are responsible for protecting your device, passwords, and backups.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
