Privacy Policy

Charging Power

Thank you for using Charging Power. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email, phone number, or contacts.

The App only accesses system-level information provided by Android, including:

  • Battery status (charging state, level, temperature, capacity, charge cycles).
  • Charging details (voltage, current, power).
  • Device information (manufacturer, model, Android version, CPU/GPU details, memory usage).

This information is displayed locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

2. How We Use Information

The information collected from your device is used only for the purpose of displaying charging and hardware details inside the App. We do not track you or use your information for advertising or analytics.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not share your information with third parties.
  • No personal or device information is uploaded to any external server.

4. Permissions

The App may require certain Android permissions to function correctly, such as:

  • Battery statistics & system info – to read charging current, voltage, temperature.
  • Storage (optional) – only if you export logs or reports.

These permissions are used solely for displaying information to you and are not misused.

5. Security

Since the App does not transmit or store personal data, your information stays safely on your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected here with a new “Last updated” date.
