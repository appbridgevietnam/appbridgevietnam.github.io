Privacy Policy

Shopping List

Thank you for using Shopping List. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email, phone number, contacts, location, or device identifiers.

All data you enter (e.g., product names, shopping lists) is stored locally on your device for your personal use. We do not transmit this data to any server.

2. How We Use Information

All information you enter is used solely for displaying and managing your shopping lists. We do not track you, analyze your data, or use any information for advertising or analytics.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not share your information with third parties.
  • No data is uploaded to cloud servers or remote services.

4. Permissions

The App does not request any special permissions such as:

  • Camera
  • Location (GPS)
  • Contacts
  • Microphone

If the App requests basic storage access in the future (e.g., for backup or export), it will only be used to store data on your device and never shared.

5. Security

Since the App does not transmit or store personal data online, your information remains securely on your device. You are responsible for protecting your device and any backups you create.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal data. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be displayed in this page with a new “Last updated” date.
