Privacy Policy

App Usage Statistics

Thank you for using App Usage Statistics. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

The app does not collect or store personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, or device identifiers.

All usage statistics analyzed by the app remain on your device and are used only to generate screen time reports and usage insights.

2. How We Use Information

The app analyzes application usage statistics on your device to display information such as:

  • Screen time
  • Usage time per app
  • App launches
  • Daily and weekly usage reports

All calculations and reports are processed locally on your device.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not collect personal data.
  • We do not upload usage data to external servers.
  • We do not share information with third parties.

4. Permissions

The app requires several system permissions in order to function properly:

  • Usage Access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS)
    Allows the app to read application usage statistics in order to measure screen time and generate usage reports.
  • Query Installed Apps (QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES)
    Allows the app to detect installed applications so that usage statistics can be displayed for each app.
  • Interact Across Profiles (INTERACT_ACROSS_PROFILES)
    Allows the app to read usage statistics across multiple profiles (such as work profiles) when available on the device.
  • Internet (INTERNET)
    Used only to load online resources such as the privacy policy page. The app does not transmit personal or usage data.

5. Security

All usage data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. This helps ensure that your usage information stays private and secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app does not collect personal information from users of any age, including children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page with a revised date.
