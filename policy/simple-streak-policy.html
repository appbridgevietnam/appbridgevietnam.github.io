Privacy Policy

Habit Tracker

This Privacy Policy describes how the Habit Tracker mobile application handles information when you use the app. We value user privacy and aim to keep the app simple, secure, and transparent.

1. Information Collection

Habit Tracker does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location data, or device identifiers.

All habit data created inside the app (such as daily completions, streaks, and progress statistics) is stored locally on your device.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Information you enter in the app is used only to provide the core functionality of the application, including:

  • Tracking your habits
  • Calculating streaks
  • Displaying statistics and progress

The app does not analyze or transmit your data to external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

Habit Tracker may display advertisements through third-party providers such as Google AdMob. These services may collect anonymous data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

  • Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties. All information remains on your device unless you choose to export or back up your data.

5. Permissions

Habit Tracker is designed to work without requiring sensitive permissions. The app does not request access to:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Location services

6. Security

Since all information remains stored locally on your device, you maintain full control of your data. Please ensure that your device is properly secured.

7. Children’s Privacy

Habit Tracker does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
