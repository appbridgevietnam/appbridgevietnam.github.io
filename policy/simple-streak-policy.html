Privacy Policy

Simple Streak

Thank you for using Simple Streak. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Simple Streak does not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, or device identifiers.

All data you enter (such as habits, streak history, and completion records) is stored locally on your device for your personal use only. We do not transmit this data to any server.

2. How We Use Information

All information you enter is used solely to display and manage your habits, track your streaks, and show your progress inside the app.

We do not track your behavior, analyze your personal data, or use any information for analytics purposes.

3. Advertising

The app may display advertisements provided by third-party services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect anonymous usage data according to their own privacy policies.

You can learn more here:

  • Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or share your personal data.
  • No user data is uploaded to our servers.
  • All habit data remains on your device.

5. Permissions

Simple Streak does not require sensitive permissions such as:

  • Camera
  • Location (GPS)
  • Contacts
  • Microphone

The app is designed to work without accessing sensitive information on your device.

6. Security

Since the app stores data locally and does not transmit personal information to external servers, your data remains under your control on your device.

7. Children’s Privacy

Simple Streak does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is safe to use for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated date.
