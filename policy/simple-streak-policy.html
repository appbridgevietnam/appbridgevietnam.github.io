Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Simple Streak ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Simple Streak mobile application handles information.

Information We Collect

Simple Streak is designed to work completely offline. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

All habit data you create is stored locally on your device.

Personal Data

We do not collect personal data such as:

  • Location

Data Storage

All habit tracking information is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your data to any servers.

Children's Privacy

Simple Streak does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
