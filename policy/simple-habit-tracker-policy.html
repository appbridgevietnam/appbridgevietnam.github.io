Privacy Policy

Simple Habit Tracker

This Privacy Policy explains how the Simple Habit Tracker mobile application handles user information. Your privacy is important to us, and the app is designed to keep your data secure and private.

1. Information Collection

Simple Habit Tracker does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, or device identifiers.

All habit data you create in the app (such as habits, streaks, and completion history) is stored locally on your device.

2. How Information Is Used

The data you enter in the app is used only to provide the core functionality of Simple Habit Tracker, including:

  • Tracking daily habits
  • Calculating streaks
  • Displaying progress and statistics

Your data is not transmitted to any external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may display advertisements from third-party services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect anonymous information according to their own privacy policies.

  • Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or distribute user data to third parties. All information remains stored on your device.

5. Permissions

Simple Habit Tracker is designed to operate without requiring sensitive permissions. The app does not request access to:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Location services

6. Security

Since all information is stored locally on your device, you maintain full control of your personal data. Please keep your device secure to protect your information.

7. Children’s Privacy

Simple Habit Tracker does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any updates will be posted on this page with the updated date.
