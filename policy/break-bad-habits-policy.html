Privacy Policy

Break Bad Habits

Thank you for using Break Bad Habits. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, or device identifiers.

All data you enter (such as habits, reset history, time tracking, and money tracking) is stored locally on your device for your personal use only. We do not transmit this data to any server.

2. How We Use Information

All information you enter is used solely to display and manage your habits, progress, and reports inside the app.

We do not track your behavior, analyze your personal data, or use any information for advertising or analytics purposes.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not share your information with any third parties.
  • No data is uploaded to cloud servers or remote services.

4. Permissions

The app does not require sensitive permissions such as:

  • Camera
  • Location (GPS)
  • Contacts
  • Microphone

If the app requests basic storage access in the future (for example, for backup or export features), it will only be used to store data on your device and will never be shared.

5. Security

Since the app does not transmit or store personal data online, your information remains on your device. You are responsible for protecting your device and any backups you create.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal data. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated date.
